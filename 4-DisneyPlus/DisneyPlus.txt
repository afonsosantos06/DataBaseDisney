Movies and series in the channel Disney Plus
Source: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/shivamb/disney-movies-and-tv-shows

Columns
show_id
type - Movie or TV Show
title
director - sometimes there are several names, that should be separated
cast - sometimes there are several names, that should be separated 
country - countries where the item is available, should be separated
date_added - date of availability in the TV channel
release_year - date of original publication
rating
duration
listed_in - categories (should be separated)
description
